Start children off on the way they should go, and even when they are old they will not turn from it. (Proverbs 22:6)

responsibility to train, teach, counsel and direct them in the

ways of God who has entrusted them to our care. We provide

a safe, clean, secured, and well-monitored environment. We

ensure that each child is taken care of – both spiritually and naturally.



We provide:

Ministry of the Word of Faith to children in ways they understand

Trained church staff and volunteers

Modern teaching aids
